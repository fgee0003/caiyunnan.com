Our story

Established in 2008, Colourful Yunnan Pty Ltd has developed from the rice noodle house on Flinders Lane to a group of 4 current restaurants in Carlton, Burwood and Hawthorn. As a leader in Yunnan Dian Cuisine restaurants, Colourful Yunnan has devoted to promoting the Dian Cuisine and its culture to the world, by allowing oversea compatriots enjoying authentic Dian Cuisine and by developing the popularity of Dian Cuisine among foreign communities.
